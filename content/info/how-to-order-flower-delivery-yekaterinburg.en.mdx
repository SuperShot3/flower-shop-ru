**Standard bouquets:** You do **not** need to arrange payment in chat before ordering. On our [website](/en/catalog) you add bouquets to the cart, enter delivery details, and click **Place order**. Online payment (YooKassa) is being set up — after your order is created, we **contact you** to confirm availability and payment. **If you enter an email at checkout**, our system can send an **order confirmation email** with your order ID, delivery recap, line items, total, and a link to **view full order details** online. Messaging us on [WhatsApp or email](/en/contact) after placing the order is **optional** unless you want to add a note or ask something. We may **contact you** (call or message) if we cannot find the address or need to clarify delivery — so a correct phone number helps. **Please add a Yandex Maps link** in the optional field at checkout when you can; it makes a real difference for the courier. For **custom work**, you may [start on our contact page](/en/contact) first.

## Overview

1. Choose a bouquet on the [website](/en/catalog)
2. Add to cart and complete checkout — **email** (recommended, for confirmation mail), **delivery zone**, **address**, date/time, driver notes, and **strongly recommended:** a **Yandex Maps link** (share URL from the Yandex Maps app)
3. Click **Place order** — your order is saved in our system (online payment coming soon via YooKassa)
4. We confirm details and payment with you by phone, WhatsApp, or email; **if you gave an email at checkout**, you may also receive our **confirmation email** (order number, summary, and link to your order)
5. **Messaging us is optional** — only if you want to add something. We may contact **you** if we need help finding the place
6. Delivery during working hours **09:00–20:00**

## Step-by-step

### 1) Order on the website

Browse [bouquets](/en/catalog), add what you need to the cart, then complete checkout with:

- Delivery zone (Yekaterinburg district or nearby city shown at checkout)
- Recipient name (and phone if available)
- **Your email (recommended)** — we can send the **order confirmation** here after the order is created (order ID, delivery summary, items, total, link to your order page)
- Delivery address (home / office / hotel / hospital)
- **Yandex Maps link (optional but strongly recommended)** — in checkout, paste a share link so the pin matches the drop-off; open Yandex Maps → drop a pin / choose the place → Share → copy link → paste into the field
- Delivery date and preferred time window
- Note for the courier (building, entrance, floor, apartment, intercom, landmarks, parking)
- Message card text (optional)
- How we can reach you (phone, WhatsApp, Telegram, or MAX)

When the form is complete, click **Place order**. You will land on an **order confirmation** page with your reference and status. **If you entered an email**, you may also get our **confirmation email** with the same essentials and a **view order** link — check inbox (and spam) for **“Order confirmation … — Lanna Bloom”**. If you skip email, rely on the page we show after checkout and your phone for any follow-up from us.

### 2) Do you have to message us after placing the order?

**No — not for a standard web order.** Checkout gives us what we need to prepare delivery. You **do not** need to open WhatsApp just to “confirm” a normal catalog order.

**When we might contact you:** If the address is unclear, the pin is wrong, or the courier cannot reach the building, we may **call or message you** using the number you provided — please answer if you can.

**When you may want to reach us:** To confirm payment before we start preparation, ask about timing, or add a special request — [WhatsApp](/en/contact), phone, or [email](/en/contact). Include your **order number or link** so we can find you quickly.

**Best thing for delivery:** Put **courier notes** and a **Yandex Maps link** in checkout up front — that usually prevents issues before we need to call.

For our [Refund, Cancellation & Replacement Policy](/en/refund-replacement), read here.

## Same-day delivery and the 18:00 cutoff

We deliver during working hours **09:00–20:00**. Same-day delivery is possible when you order early (subject to bouquet availability and courier capacity).

Orders placed **after 18:00** are usually scheduled for the **next day**. Sometimes same-day is still possible, but it depends on stock and the delivery schedule.

If it is urgent, complete checkout as early as you can; add a **Yandex Maps link** and clear courier notes so we can go straight to the pin.

## FAQ

### 1) I placed an order on the website — what should I do next?

**Wait for our confirmation** or check your **order page** / **confirmation email** (if you provided an email at checkout). We will contact you about payment and any details we need. Message us **only if** you want to change something or ask a question — include your order number or link. We may contact **you** if we need help with the address.

### 2) Can I edit delivery details after ordering?

Often yes, if we have not started preparation or dispatched the courier. Contact us with your order number or link as soon as possible.

### 3) Can you deliver to hotels, offices, or hospitals?

Yes. Please include room, ward, or reception details if available, plus any access rules (lobby, reception, intercom, gate code). A **Yandex Maps link** to the correct entrance helps a lot. Hospitals may have delivery restrictions — put details in checkout and we will contact you if something cannot be done.

### 4) Can I choose an exact delivery time?

You can choose a preferred time window. Exact timing depends on traffic and delivery routing, but we follow your window as closely as possible.

### 5) Can I add a message card?

Yes. Add it at checkout or send the text when you contact us. Keep it clear and short for the card.

### 6) What if the courier has trouble finding the address?

This is why **Yandex Maps link + written address + courier notes** in checkout matter — we try to fix it before dispatch. If something is still unclear, we **contact you** on the phone number you gave. You can also message us if you want to add a landmark after ordering.

### 7) What proof of payment do you need?

Until YooKassa is live, we confirm payment individually (for example bank transfer). Your **order page** and **confirmation email** (when you gave an email) show your order reference. Send a payment screenshot in WhatsApp or email only if we asked for it. If something looks wrong, contact us with your order number or link.

### 8) Changes, cancellations, refunds

If something changes, contact us as soon as you can with your order number or link. Policy details are here: [Refund / Replacement / Cancellation Policy](/en/refund-replacement).
